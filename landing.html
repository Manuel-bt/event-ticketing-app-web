How it works

From poster to
scanned entry.

The complete journey — for you and your guests — in three steps.

01
You list your event
Fill in the name, venue, date, price. Hit publish. Your event appears on the TicketBure map with a price pin — visible to everyone browsing Dar es Salaam events right now.
Takes 30 seconds
02
Guests buy with Mobile Money
They tap your pin on the map, tap Buy, and get an instant STK Push on their phone. They enter their M-Pesa PIN. Done. A QR code ticket lands in their wallet — no bank card needed.
Under 30 seconds to buy
03
You scan them in at the door
Open your Organizer Dashboard on any smartphone. Point the camera at their QR code. Green means in, red means fake. No card machine, no printed list, no "let me check my spreadsheet."
Works offline too
